Peer-to-Peer Community Relief

Due to the current health crisis many families are currently displaced from work making it hard for them to provide for their loved ones. During an unprecendented time like this, no one deserves to go hungry. This is an initiative to provide economic relief to those families by anonymous and peer-to-peer donations of non-perishable goods.

How it works:

Volunteer

Volunteers pick up donations outside the location of the person donating and drops them off to the person in need.

Donate

After a request is made for certain donations, the goods most in demand are posted and people are able to donate any extra unopened non-perishable foods they may have.

In-Need

Those in need of food or other supplies can anonymously request their needs and receive them in a timely peroid with no financial burden.
